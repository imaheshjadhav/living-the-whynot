The Philosophy

"I don't plan,
I just do.
I'd rather suffer than
miss out."

Comfort is a slow death. I’ve spent my 20s checking boxes that didn’t belong to me. Now, I chase the friction. The awkward silences, the missed flights, and the stories that only happen when you say 'Yes' to the wrong direction.

Being 32 isn't about stability; it's about finally having the courage to be reckless with intent.

THE LEFT TURN
IN GOA.

Everyone goes south for the beaches. I took a left into the jungle because a man with one tooth told me there was a temple that didn't exist on Google Maps. Four days later, I emerged with no phone, a stained shirt, and the best story of my life.
